Privacy Policy

1. Scope

This policy explains how Docivo handles information when you use the mobile app, including scanning, OCR, PDF editing, signatures, and sharing.

2. Data We Process

Depending on how you use the app, Docivo may process scan images, PDFs, OCR text, folder names, document titles, signatures, and app settings.

3. Local-First Processing

The current build is local-first. Documents are stored in app-local storage on your device. By default, Docivo does not require account creation and does not upload your documents to Docivo servers.

4. Sharing and Exports

When you tap share or export, files are handed to the destination app or provider you select. Their privacy practices then apply to the shared copy.

5. Permissions

  • Camera permission is used for document scanning.
  • File/media access is used only for import, export, or share actions you request.

6. Payments

If premium purchases are enabled, billing data is processed by Google Play or Apple App Store. Docivo does not receive full card details from those stores.

7. Retention and Deletion

Deleted items move to Recently deleted until restored or permanently removed. Permanent deletion removes local files from app storage where possible.

8. Security

Docivo relies on platform sandboxing and operating system security controls. You should also use device lock, encryption, and secure backups.

9. Children

Docivo is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required by local law).

10. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access, correction, or deletion of personal information in Docivo control. For local-only data, you can also delete it directly in the app.

11. Policy Updates

Docivo may update this policy as features or legal requirements change.
